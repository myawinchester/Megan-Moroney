About

Megan Ann Moroney, born in Savannah, Georgia, is a rising star in country music, known for her candid songwriting and "emo cowgirl" style. After graduating from the University of Georgia, she moved to Nashville, where her career took off with the viral hit "Tennessee Orange" in 2022. Her debut album, "Lucky," and its follow-up, "Am I Okay?", have cemented her as a fresh, honest voice in the genre, earning her multiple award nominations and a dedicated fan base.
